Engineer

def- A person who wants to create a better world

From an early age, I was always curious about the world around me. I wondered about how things work; from bridges to more mundane things like walking, but I also wondered about why there are so much injustices in the world? In our modern age, where launching rockets are a regular routine, why do we still have world hunger, water shortages, and basic human necessities? There might not be a panacea for everything, but as engineers, it is our job to try our hardest to find one.
