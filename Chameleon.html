Chameleon Game

This project was for our Design Nature class, where  we design and create a bio-inspired game for kids. As a team of 4, we decided to create a game based on the mechanisms of a chameleon! My jobs on the team were creating the tongue mechanism, 3D printing all relevant parts, and helping with creating the paper mache chameleon heads.

In this project, I got lots of experience CADing complex moving mechanisms and 3D printing. I really liked how the end product came out!
